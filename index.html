About Me

My journey to becoming a full stack developer.

  • 2011-2014

    East Coast PM & SEM

    I grew up on the East Coast and started my career working for an award-winning web design company as a Project Manager and Marketer.


    There I managed a wide range of agile projects, wrangled Google Analytics, and improved quantity and quality of leads for clients. I also gave talks at Biz Conferences and Drupal Camps in New England, and spent as much time near the ocean as possible.

  • August 2014

    Welcome to Colorful Colorado

    Ready for a change of pace, I packed up and moved to Denver. Beautiful mountains, booming tech scene, and plenty of sunshine... what's not to love? When not out enjoying all that Colorado has to offer, I continue to work as a Web Specialist and SEM consultant while honing my skills as a developer.

  • August 2015

    Testing the Waters

    To be sure I was truly passionate about coding before committing to an intensive bootcamp AND career shift, I completed Codeacademy courses in HTML/CSS and Javascript. Then I was hooked!

  • November 2015

    Coding Dojo Begins

    I applied to Coding Dojo with 100 hours of coding under my belt, and after being accepted I dove in head-first. Thus began my 5 month immersion into all things codeâ€¦ and an increase in coffee intake.
